About the association

The Association of Legal Entities "ONALTU National Association of Rehabilitation Centers" is a professional community created to develop and improve the medical rehabilitation system in Kazakhstan.

We are bringing together the country's leading rehabilitation centers, working on the implementation of international standards and creating a single space for the exchange of experience and professional growth of specialists.

Our partners

MISSION, VALUES AND GOALS

MISSION

Unification of rehabilitation services of the Republic of Kazakhstan through the standards of the best international practices in providing rehabilitation assistance.

VALUES

  • Transparency
  • Collaborative Culture
  • Equality
  • Continuous improvement
  • Continuous learning

GOALS

Coordination, representation and protection of the common, including professional interests of its members, coordination and development of medical rehabilitation and rehabilitation treatment for children and adults of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

Frequently Asked Questions

To join, you must apply through our website, submit your organization's registration documents, and undergo an assessment of compliance with the basic requirements of the association.

The cost of membership depends on the size of your organization and the amount of services provided. We offer a flexible membership fee system designed to accommodate the capabilities of organizations of different sizes.

The members of the association undertake to comply with the Code of ethics, participate in annual general meetings, pay membership fees on time and strive to implement recommended quality standards.
