History of creation

Our association is designed to become an experimental platform for the development, monitoring, research, analysis, testing, implementation and implementation of modern models of rehabilitation care.

We bring together the best rehabilitation centers in the country to create uniform standards of medical care based on international best practices.

MANAGEMENT BODIES

General Meeting

The Supreme Governing Body

The highest governing body of the association, which has exclusive competence in resolving key issues of the organization's activities.

President of the Association

Senior official

He is elected by the decision of the General Meeting of the Association for a term of 5 (five) years. Carries out the general management of the association's activities and represents its interests.

The Control and Auditing Commission

The control body

Monitors the financial and economic activities of the association, compliance with the statutory requirements and the implementation of decisions of the General Meeting.

The norm of representation:

Each member of the Association has the right to one representative with the right to one vote at the General Meeting.

Our vision

We strive to create an integrated rehabilitation care system that combines the best domestic and international practices, ensures the availability of quality services for all residents of Kazakhstan and will become a model for other countries in the region.

Development and implementation of uniform quality standards
Creation of a continuous training system for specialists
Introduction of innovative rehabilitation technologies
Development of scientific research in the field of rehabilitation
